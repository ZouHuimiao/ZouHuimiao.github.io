音符

A musical note has several parts, including a note head, stem, and flag, that together indicate the duration (length) of the note. Notes of shorter duration than a quarter note have flags, and groups of shorter notes are joined together with beams.

一个音符由几部分组成，包括符头，符干和符尾，共同表示了一个音符的延音长度。延音长度比四分音符还短的音符具有符尾，一组连续的短音符通过"横杠"相连。

w h q e x

Each note shown above is half the duration (length) of the note to its left. From left to right, the notes are whole note, half note, quarter note, eighth note, and sixteenth note.

上述的音符相对于它左侧的音符都要少一半的延音。从左往右分别是，全音符，半音符，四分音符，八分音符，十六分音符。
