音符(Notes)

A musical note has several parts, including a note head, stem, and flag, that together indicate the duration (length) of the note. Notes of shorter duration than a quarter note have flags, and groups of shorter notes are joined together with beams.

一个音符由几部分组成，包括符头，符干和符尾，共同表示了一个音符的延音长度。延音长度比四分音符还短的音符具有符尾，一组连续的短音符通过"横杠"相连。

w h q e x

Each note shown above is half the duration (length) of the note to its left. From left to right, the notes are whole note, half note, quarter note, eighth note, and sixteenth note.

上述的音符相对于它左侧的音符都要少一半的延音。从左往右分别是，全音符，半音符，四分音符，八分音符，十六分音符。

休止符(Rests)

When you play music, the spaces between notes are just as important as the notes themselves. The space between notes are shown using rests. Rests, like notes, have different symbols for different lengths of time, and shorter rests use flags.

当你演奏音乐时，音符之间的时间间隙同音符本身一样重要。这些时间间隙使用休止符来表示。休止符和音符一样，不同时间长度有不同的符号进行表示，短的休止符也使用符尾。

带点的音符(Dotted Notes)

w. = h + q

A dot after a note or rest increases its length by half (50%). For example, since a half note lasts two beats, a dotted half note lasts (2+1) three beats.

在音符或者休止符后面加一个点，表示时间上要再延长50%。比如，一个半音符持续两拍，若后面带点表示其持续(2+1)三拍。

Dotted notes are handy when playing in a time signature with 3 beats to the measure, such as 3/4, and also for syncopated rhythms, with some notes occurring off of the beat.
